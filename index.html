FAQ

What is phishing?
Phishing is when cybercriminals create fake websites, emails, or pop-ups that look exactly like real ones — your bank, PayPal, Amazon, Google — to trick you into typing your passwords, credit card numbers, or personal information. It's the #1 cybercrime in the world and it happens 3.4 billion times every day.
How does NøFishing AI protect me?
The moment you land on any website, our AI engine runs 14 simultaneous checks — analyzing the URL structure, SSL certificate, domain age, redirect chains, visual mimicry patterns, and more. If anything looks suspicious, we block it instantly and show you a full-screen warning before you can type a single character. You don't have to do anything. It just works.
Is my sensitive information safe?
Yes. NøFishing AI protects everything scammers are after. Your passwords, personal details, financial information, social media accounts, email credentials, crypto wallets, medical records, and business logins. Phishing attacks target every corner of your digital life — your identity, your money, your privacy. NøFishing AI blocks the fake sites trying to steal it all, before you ever see them.
Does NøFishing AI collect my data?
Never. We analyze website data, not your data. We don't store your browsing history, passwords, personal information, or anything you type. Ever. Our only job is to keep you safe — not to monetize you.
What happens when a phishing site is detected?
You'll see an immediate full-screen red warning that takes over your browser. It tells you the site is dangerous and gives you one option — get out. No confusion, no subtle notifications you might miss. It's impossible to ignore by design.
Do I need to update NøFishing AI manually?
No. Our AI model updates continuously in the background. New phishing threats emerge every hour — our system learns and adapts automatically so you're always protected against the latest attacks without lifting a finger.
Which browsers are supported?
NøFishing AI is available for Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. Install it in one click directly from your browser's extension store — no account needed to install, no technical setup, no configuration. It starts protecting you the second it's installed.
Is NøFishing AI available on mobile?
Not yet. NøFishing AI currently protects you on desktop browsers. Mobile protection is on our roadmap and coming soon.
